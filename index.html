What's an NFT?

An NFT is a "Non-fungible Token"... a unique crypto token that can't be duplicated. It's basically a public certificate of ownership on a blockchain.


You may have heard of them on the news with some selling for tens of millions of dollars or of Twitter's founder making and selling an NFT of his first tweet.

What's BitSig?

BitSig enables new types of NFTs that can be signed. Since NFTs can't be duplicated, they act like physical objects. Just like physical objects can be signed, an NFT should be signable too.


With BitSig, you can create NFTs that are signable for free by anyone. If someone famous signs your NFT, it becomes more valuable just like a soccer ball does when your favorite soccer player signs it.


If you sign someone else's NFT, you can get a percentage of the NFT's sale, every time it's sold.

How do I sign an NFT?

Signing an NFT with BitSig is very easy. Simply scan the QR code of the token with the app and it automatically signs it.


If you don't have the app, you can connect your wallet to this website and sign it here.


There's no cost to sign anything and it only takes a matter of seconds. When the token you signed is sold, you can get a percentage of the sale deposited to your BitSig app.

Why did we create it?

There is no existing way to sign NFT's easily and for free. Your favorite artist can sign your shirt, but not your NFT. With BitSig, we want to bring signatures over to the virtual world.

If you're a fan...

Create fan art for your favorite artist to sign for free. The NFT with your art gets valuable when your favorite artist signs it. If you choose to sell it, you can support the artist by giving them a portion of the sale automatically.

If you're an artist/creator...

Connect with your fans by creating an NFT for them to sign. When you sell the NFT to a super fan, you can give back to the community of fans that supported you by giving them a portion of the sale.
